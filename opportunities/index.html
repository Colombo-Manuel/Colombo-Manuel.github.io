Join Us

Join Us

I am always enthusiastic about working with creative, interdisciplinary and self‑motivated students and postdoctoral fellows who are passionate about geoscience and nature at large. Given the diverse research topics studied in the B.A.B. Lab, there is always room for interesting collaborations! Having a diverse, inclusive and welcoming Lab is something I strive for, and hence, I encourage underrepresented students and postdocs within Geosciences from the USA and abroad to contact me. If you are a motivated student / researcher with a strong science background, please do contact me to explore potential collaborations! Please, include your CV and a brief description of your interests and their relationship with the research done at the B.A.B. Lab.

When funding is available, I will be actively recruiting MSc and Ph.D. students and postdoctoral researchers (Current Opportunities), however, I strongly encourage those students and postdoctoral researcher who want to apply for external fellowships to discuss with me potential projects in advance. See below for information about fellowships.


The Virginia Institute of Marine Science, located in the beautiful Chesapeake Bay, and William & Mary University are great places for those looking for a high quality education and research opportunities in the realms of Ocean, Coastal and Estuarine Science. Prospective MSc and Ph.D. students please check out the School of Marine Science (SMS), SMS: Academic & General Policies and this Link to get more information about the graduate programs, admission and timeline.

Current Funded Opportunities

Unfortunately, we currently do not have any funded opportunity. I update this page regularly posting new positions when available. However, I strongly advise you to take a look at the fellowships listed below as they can be a way to start your path at VIMS and the B.A.B. Lab. We always have interesting projects to work on and expand upon!

Scholarship and Fellowship Information for Prospective Students and Postdocs

Regardless of whether you are planning to apply for undergraduate or postdoctoral scholarships, the sooner you contact me to discuss potential projects and steps to follow the better! Please allow yourself at least a three month period before the deadline to contact me and work on your application.

Below you will find some scholarships and fellowships targeted from undergraduate students to postdoctoral researcher (US citizens and foreigners). However, there are many other private and state sponsored fellowships that are not included in this list.

Undergraduate Students:

  • VIMS REU Program
  • NOAA - Ernest F. Hollings Undergraduate Scholarship – U.S. citizens and/or Permanent Residents

Graduate Students:

  • NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program – U.S. citizens and/or Permanent Residents
  • Quad Fellowship – Citizens or legal permanent residents of Australia, India, Japan, or the United States
  • Fulbright U.S. Student Program – Plase visit the Fulbright division from your own country; for example Fulbright Australia, Fulbright Mexico, Fulbright Chile, Fulbright Argentina, etc.
  • Schlumberger Foundation Faculty for the Future Program - Women from developing countries pursuing PhD and postdoctoral research STEM disciplines.
  • Virginia Sea Grant Graduate Fellowships - Open to full-time graduate students at any Virginia academic institution who are engaged in coastal and marine research relevant to Virginia and the Virginia Sea Grant strategic plan
  • The Paul & Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans - Immigrants and children of immigrants to the United States; up to $90,000 in financial support over two years in any field and in any advanced degree-granting program in the United States.

Postdoctoral Researchers:

  • VIMS RAISE Post-Doctoral Fellowship Program – U.S. citizens, permanent residents and foreign citizens are eligible to apply RAISE 2023 application Ad
  • Various NSF Postdoctoral Research Fellowships – U.S. citizens or Permanent Residents
  • NSERC Postdoctoral Fellowship Program - Canadian citizens or permanent residents who completed their Ph.D. at a Canadian Institutions
  • Banting Postdoctoral Fellowships - Canadian citizens or permanent residents who completed their Ph.D. at a Canadian Institutions
  • Agouron Institute Geobiology Fellowship
  • The Branco Weiss Fellowship
  • The Ford Foundation Fellowships
  • Schlumberger Foundation Faculty for the Future Program - Women from developing countries pursuing PhD and postdoctoral research STEM disciplines.
  • The L’Oréal USA For Women in Science fellowship program - U.S. citizens or Permanent Residents
